About Us

Over 30 years protecting what matters most

Saimon Group, a leading US based company in comprehensive technology solutions for security, with presence throughout the American continent.

With integrity at the core

Technology with purpose

In an environment that demands immediate and intelligent responses, we incorporate the most advanced Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Generative AI to create systems that transform the way emergencies are prevented, managed and resolved.

Our solutions process large volumes of data in seconds, identify non-visible patterns and anticipate risks, providing our clients with strategic tools to act with precision, efficiency and confidence.

Our legacy
+30 years
of experience in security technology

Saimon Group represents an evolution to address the need to analyze massive amounts of raw data to empower decision makers. Our experience is nurtured by decades building over 188 real time crime centers, which today provide the full range of public safety services for over 22 million people in some of the most challenging locations of the Western hemisphere.

Our history

1998

The first steps

Founded as Seguritech by Simon Picker, beginning electronic security operations.

2005

First C4 center

Construction of the first Command, Control, Communications and Computing Center for state government.

2012

National expansion

Presence in over 20 states with video surveillance infrastructure and monitoring centers.

2019

A new vision

Transition to Saimon Group, integrating AI, Business Intelligence and Robotics as a single ecosystem.

2023

International expansion

Consolidated operations in Mexico, the United States and Colombia with over 188 projects delivered.

Today

An expanding ecosystem

Robotics, AI and data analytics converging into a single ecosystem for any industry.
