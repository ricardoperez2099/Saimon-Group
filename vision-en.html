Video analytics with real intelligence

In a connected global environment, what is invisible is most dangerous. Saimon Vision turns video surveillance into business intelligence.

From passive surveillance to active intelligence

People, vehicles, industries and services move at the same time within increasingly complex systems, where risks often hide in the everyday: unusual behavior, access outside protocol, an operational anomaly that goes unnoticed.

Saimon Vision does not just store video. Through advanced artificial intelligence models, it turns video into structured text, processes the information, relates data and transforms isolated recordings into clear, actionable answers.
